E-Bike Adventures in the Heart of Germany!

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Bavarian Alps on an unforgettable e-mountain bike tour! Explore stunning trails, conquer epic climbs with ease, and soak in panoramic views.

Whether you're a seasoned rider or new to e-biking, we've got the perfect adventure for you. Bring your own e-bike or rent one of our top-of-the-line models. Book your Alpine escape today and discover the magic of e-biking!

Our Services

Guided E-Bike Tours

Offer a variety of guided tours for different skill levels and interests, showcasing the best trails and scenery in the region.

E-Bike Rentals

Provide a fleet of high-quality, well-maintained mountain e-bikes for members and visitors to rent.

Skills Clinics and Workshops

Host clinics and workshops to help riders improve their e-biking skills, including bike handling, trail etiquette, and basic maintenance.

Bike Maintenance and Repair

Offer on-site bike maintenance and repair services to keep members' bikes in top condition, or to fix any issues that arise during a tour.

Social Events and Rides

Organize regular social rides, events, and gatherings to foster a sense of community among e-bike enthusiasts.

Route Planning and Information

Provide detailed information about local trails, including maps, difficulty ratings, and points of interest.

Accommodation Partnerships

Partner with local hotels, guesthouses, or cabins to offer members discounted accommodation packages for multi-day trips.

Gear Sales and Rental

Offer essential gear and accessories for sale or rent, including helmets, gloves, GPS devices, and more.
